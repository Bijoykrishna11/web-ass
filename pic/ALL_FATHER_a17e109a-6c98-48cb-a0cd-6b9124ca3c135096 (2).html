In order to turn off the headlights after 15 seconds time interval  a circuit is to be implemented in which  Ignition switch is off and Light switch is on. For this  we need a timer which would produce a LOW after 15 seconds time interval. This will be the input to the headlight control circuit.

We need an AND gate to operate when one of its inputs is LOW (Ignition switch off) and one input is HIGH (Light switch is on). So, we need an inverter at one of the inputs to the AND gate where the logic level is LOW (i.e. at the signal from ignition switch).

The output of the AND gate activates the timer to produce a LOW signal after15 second, which is fed to the headlight control circuit.
